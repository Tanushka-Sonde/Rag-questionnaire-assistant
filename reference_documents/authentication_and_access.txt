Authentication and Access Management

StreamHive supports multiple authentication methods for users including email and password login, OAuth-based social login, and enterprise single sign-on (SSO) integrations.

Administrator and internal staff accounts are required to use multi-factor authentication (MFA) for accessing internal dashboards and infrastructure management systems.

Passwords are securely stored using bcrypt hashing with salted hashes to prevent unauthorized recovery.

Access permissions across the platform are managed through role-based access control, ensuring that users and administrators only have access to resources necessary for their role.
