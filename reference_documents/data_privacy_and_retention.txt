Data Privacy and Retention Policy

StreamHive collects limited personal information necessary for account creation and service delivery. This includes user email address, subscription details, and viewing preferences.

User viewing history and activity data are retained for up to 24 months to support recommendation systems and improve content discovery.

If a user account is deleted, associated personal data is removed or anonymized within 30 days, unless retention is required for legal or regulatory obligations.

StreamHive does not sell personal user data to third parties and applies strict privacy protection standards for all stored user information.
